About SDG 14 - Life Below Water

SDG 14 is one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals by the United Nations, part of the 2030 Agenda. It focuses on conserving and sustainably using the oceans, seas, and marine resources for a better and healthier future.

Marine pollution, plastic waste, and climate change are destroying ocean ecosystems. Our website spreads awareness and encourages people to protect marine life.

The Problems We Fight

  • Plastic Pollution: 11 Million Metric Tons of plastic enter ocean yearly
  • Overfishing: 35% of global fish stocks are overfished
  • Climate Change: Ocean warming, rising sea levels
